Why Enejistats Exists

Across Nigeria and many parts of Africa, thousands of talented footballers remain unseen by the professional world — not because they lack skill, but because they lack visibility. Traditional scouting is limited, and most grassroots players have no structured way to present their match performance, career growth, or stats in a standardized and trusted format.

Addressing Talent Underservice

Most players at the grassroots level have little or no access to professional analytics, scouting reports, or even digital performance records. Clubs and scouts often work blindly, lacking data-driven insight into emerging talent. Players, in turn, miss out on opportunities due to outdated systems and disconnected ecosystems.

The Enejistats Solution

Enejistats is a pioneering indigenous platform built for Nigerian and African players — offering tools to record, manage, and display individual match performance data in real-time. From player dashboards to scout tools, Enejistats connects the grassroots to the global stage through accessible statistics, transparent data, and structured visibility.

Our Mission

To empower football talent across Nigeria and Africa with visibility, data-driven opportunity, and a platform to be seen, scouted, and celebrated.
